Return & Replacement Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At Wallitude, all our products are custom-made, so returns are limited. Please read carefully.

1. No Return Policy

Since each product is personalized based on your uploaded images and design, we do not accept returns once the order is placed.

2. Replacement Eligibility

We offer replacements only in the following cases:

  • Damaged product upon delivery
  • Wrong product delivered
  • Manufacturing defects

3. Proof Requirement

To request a replacement, you must:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide clear photos or videos of the issue

4. Replacement Process

Once approved:

  • A replacement will be processed at no extra cost
  • No refunds will be issued — only replacements

5. Design Responsibility

Customers are responsible for:

  • Uploading correct images
  • Checking spelling, layout, and preview before placing the order

Please note: Wallitude is not responsible for errors in customer-approved designs. Please review your preview carefully before completing your order.

6. Cancellation Policy

Orders cannot be cancelled once they are processed or sent for production.
