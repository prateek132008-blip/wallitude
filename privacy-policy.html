Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At Wallitude, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our website:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Shipping address
  • Uploaded images and custom design data

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Deliver customized products
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our website and services

3. Image & Design Data

All images and designs uploaded by customers are used solely for order processing and preview generation. We do not sell or share your images with third parties.

4. Payment Security

All payments are securely processed through Razorpay. We do not store your payment details.

5. Data Storage

Your order details and uploaded content may be securely stored using Google services (Google Drive and Google Sheets) for order processing and record-keeping.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Data may only be shared with:

  • Payment providers
  • Delivery partners

7. Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us.

8. Updates to Policy

Wallitude may update this policy at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
