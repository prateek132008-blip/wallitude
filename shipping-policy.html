Shipping Policy

Last updated: April 2026

We aim to deliver your custom frames safely and on time.

2–4 Days processing
5–10 Days delivery
FREE Shipping included

1. Order Processing Time

  • Orders are processed within 2–4 business days
  • Customization and quality checks are included in this time

2. Shipping Time

  • Delivery typically takes 5–10 business days across India
  • Delivery time may vary based on location

3. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges (if applicable) will be shown at checkout.

4. Order Tracking

Tracking details will be shared once your order is shipped.

5. Delays

Delays may occur due to:

  • High order volume
  • Courier partner issues
  • Unforeseen circumstances

6. Incorrect Address

  • Customers must provide accurate shipping details
  • Wallitude is not responsible for delays or losses due to incorrect addresses

Please double-check your address, city, state, and pincode before completing your order. Incorrect details cannot be changed once the order is dispatched.

7. Delivery Issues

If your order is marked delivered but not received, contact us within 48 hours.
